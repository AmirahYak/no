who invented soccer

modern soccer began in England in 1863. However, the very earliest form of kicking a ball seems to have originated from a Chinese military manual dating back to the 2nd and 3rd centuries called Tsu' Chu. The game consisted of kicking a leather ball filled with feathers and hair through an opening, measuring only 30-40cm in width into a small next fixed into long bamboo canes.
